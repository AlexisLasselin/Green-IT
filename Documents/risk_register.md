# Risk Register

This document serves as a comprehensive risk register for our project. It identifies potential risks, their impact, likelihood, mitigation strategies.

| Risk ID | Risk Description                                         | Impact Level | Likelihood | Mitigation Strategy                                        |
| ------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | ------------ | ---------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1       | Delay in project timeline due to resource unavailability | High         | Medium     | Cross-train team members and maintain a resource buffer    |
| 2       | Budget overrun due to unforeseen expenses                | High         | Low        | Regular budget reviews and contingency planning            |
| 3       | Technical challenges with new technology implementation  | Medium       | High       | Conduct thorough research and pilot testing                |
| 4       | Regulatory changes impacting project scope               | Medium       | Medium     | Stay updated with regulatory news and maintain flexibility |
| 5       | Stakeholder dissatisfaction with project outcomes        | High         | Low        | Regular communication and feedback sessions                |
| 6       | Data security breaches                                   | High         | Medium     | Implement robust security protocols and regular audits     |
| 7       | Loss of key personnel                                    | Medium       | Low        | Succession planning and knowledge sharing                  |
| 8       | Non-compliance with industry standards                   | High         | Medium     | Regular training and audits                                |
| 9       | GDPR violations                                          | High         | Low        | Implement strict data handling policies                    |
| 10      | Outages of critical systems                              | High         | Medium     | Establish backup systems and disaster recovery plans       |
